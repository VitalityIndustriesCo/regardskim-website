# Regards Kim — 90-Second Demo Video: Script & Shot List
**For Matt to record raw footage. Jimmy will cut, caption, brand, and add music.**

## Recording setup
- Record your screen at 1920x1080 or higher (QuickTime: File → New Screen Recording, select full screen)
- Use the dev/test store with a few realistic emails seeded in the inbox
- Browser: clean window, no extra tabs/bookmarks bar (Cmd+Shift+B hides it), 100% zoom
- No talking needed — just move deliberately, pause ~2s on each state so I have cut room
- Total raw footage target: 3–4 minutes (will be cut to ~90s)

## Seed data needed before recording
- 4–6 emails in Needs reply: a WISMO ("where is my order"), a return/exchange, an order change, plus 1–2 in No reply needed (newsletter/spam)
- At least one email must match a real test order with tracking attached

## Shots (in order)

**Shot 1 — The pile (5s use):** Open the Regards Kim inbox showing the Needs reply list with several sorted emails and their tags. Hover slowly down the list.

**Shot 2 — Open the WISMO email (10s):** Click the "where is my order" email. Let the detail load fully: customer email, matched order, status, carrier, tracking. Pause 3s.

**Shot 3 — Order context (8s):** Slowly move the cursor over the order panel — order number, fulfillment status, tracking number, Order Details. Pause on the tracking link.

**Shot 4 — AI draft ready (12s):** Open the prepared draft. Let the draft display completely. Don't move the mouse while it appears. Pause 3s on the finished draft.

**Shot 5 — Edit moment (8s):** Click into the draft, change one small phrase (shows the human-in-control moment), pause.

**Shot 6 — Send (8s):** Click send. Capture the confirmation state. Pause 3s.

**Shot 7 — Back to the list (6s):** Return to the inbox showing the email now handled/cleared from Needs reply.

**Shot 8 — No reply needed tab (6s):** Click the No reply needed filter, showing the junk that was filtered out automatically.

**Shot 9 — (Optional) Saved replies or Improve reply (8s):** One more assistive feature in action if quick.

## Narrative the final cut will follow (captions, no voiceover v1)
