# 5. FAQ

## 1. What does RegardsKim do?
RegardsKim helps Shopify stores handle post-purchase customer emails. Kim prepares draft replies for order status, tracking, returns, refunds, and exchanges, so you can review and send them faster.

## 2. Does Kim send emails automatically?
Not at the moment. Kim prepares the reply and leaves it ready for your approval in Gmail, so you stay in control of what gets sent.

## 3. What kinds of emails can Kim help with?
Kim is built for post-purchase support, including order updates, tracking questions, returns, refunds, and exchanges.

## 4. Does RegardsKim work with Shopify?
Yes. RegardsKim is designed for Shopify stores and uses your store information to help prepare accurate post-purchase replies.

## 5. Do I need to switch email platforms?
No. Kim works with Gmail, so your support workflow can stay in the inbox you already use.

## 6. Is this a chatbot?
No. RegardsKim is focused on handling post-purchase support email workflow behind the scenes, not adding a chatbot to your storefront.

## 7. How much does it cost?
RegardsKim is $49 per month for one simple plan.

## 8. How does this compare to hiring a VA?
A part-time VA for inbox support often costs $200 to $500 per month, before training and oversight. RegardsKim helps with the repetitive post-purchase workload for $49/month.

## 9. Will the replies still sound like my brand?
Kim is designed to prepare clear, polished replies for your review. You can check each draft before sending and make edits as needed.

## 10. Who is RegardsKim best for?
RegardsKim is best for small to mid-sized Shopify stores that want customer support emails handled more efficiently without hiring extra admin help.
